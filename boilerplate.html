Points vs miles

  • Get as many points and miles as you can
  • Airlines issue miles
  • Banks/credit card issuers issue points
  • Hotels issue points
  • Hotel points transfer to airline miles, usually at an unfavorable ratio (i.e 10:1).
  • Exception: SPG (Starwood Hotel Group points)
  • Good credit card points transfer to airline miles, usually at a 1:1 ratio.
  • Airline miles cannot be exchanged for any other rewards currency except in specific cases
  • Specific cases: Hawaiian Miles & Virgin Atlantic miles
  • Earn & Burn

Credit

  • Your credit score is an asset
  • 3 major credit bureaus
  • Debt/credit utilization ratio + type of accounts + # of accounts + length of time accounts are open + payment history + magic = credit score
  • Keep track of it
    • creditkarma.com - transunion
    • creditsesame.com - experian
    • quizzle.com - equifax
    • freecreditreport.com - experian - actually $1
    • annualcreditreport.com - all 3, no score

Stopovers, Layovers, Open Jaws

  • Stopover - stop & get out for undefined amount of time
  • Layover - 4 hours domestically, 24 hours internationally
  • Open Jaw - example
  • Every airline rewards program has different rules
  • United is the best - 1 stopover and two open jaws
  • US Airways - 1 stopover OR 1 open jaw. Protip: Agents are geographically challenged
  • American - 1 stopover in North American gateway (complicated)
  • Delta - don't know, don't care
